Minor Test 04
Class: 6 & 7 Subject: General Mathematics Time: 1 Hour Max. Marks: 40
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly. Clearly show the steps of your calculations.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Attempt all questions - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Convert the fraction 3/4 into its decimal representation.
2.
Find the Least Common Multiple (LCM) of 4 and 6.
3.
Add the following fractions: 1/5 + 2/5
4.
Convert the decimal number 0.25 into a fraction in its simplest form.
5.
Find the Highest Common Factor (HCF) of 12 and 18.
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Simplify the given decimal expression: 2.5 + 3.2 - 1.4
7.
A piece of ribbon is 5/8 meters long. If 1/4 meters is cut from it, what is the length of the remaining ribbon?
8.
Find the Least Common Multiple (LCM) of 12, 15, and 20.
9.
Multiply the decimal numbers: 3.4 × 1.2
10.
Two numbers have an HCF of 6 and an LCM of 36. If one of the numbers is 12, find the other number.
SECTION C (15 Marks)(Long Answer Type - 5 Marks Each)
11.
Three school bells ring at intervals of 10 minutes, 15 minutes, and 20 minutes respectively. If they all ring together at 8:00 AM, at what time will they ring together again?
12.
A boy spends 1/3 of his pocket money on buying books and 1/4 of his pocket money on snacks.
(i) What fraction of his money did he spend in total?
(ii) What fraction of his pocket money is left with him?
13.
Evaluate the following:
(i) Divide 15.6 by 1.2
(ii) Find the greatest number that will divide 24, 36, and 60 exactly without leaving any remainder.
