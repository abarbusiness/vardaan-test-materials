Minor Test 04
Class: 6 ICSE Subject: Mathematics Time: 1 Hour Max. Marks: 40
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Section A contains 5 questions of 2 marks each.
  • Section B contains 5 questions of 3 marks each.
  • Section C contains 3 questions of 5 marks each.
  • Show all working steps clearly.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Attempt all questions - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Write the opposite of the following statements:
(a) A profit of ₹ 250
(b) 15 km towards South
2.
Define a 'Line Segment'. How many endpoints does a line segment have?
3.
Find the value of the following expression: $|-18| - |-6| + |2|$
4.
What are collinear points?
5.
Subtract $-15$ from $8$.
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Simplify the following integer expression: $$(-25) + 14 - (-10) + 5$$
7.
Differentiate between intersecting lines and parallel lines.
8.
The temperature at midnight was $-5^{\circ}\text{C}$. By morning, it rose by $8^{\circ}\text{C}$. What was the morning temperature? Represent the calculation using integers.
9.
Define the term 'Ray'. Give two examples of a ray from your daily life.
10.
Fill in the blanks with appropriate signs ($<$, $>$, or $=$):
(a) $(-8) + (-5)$  _______  $(-8) - (-5)$
(b) $0$  _______  $-15$
(c) $|-10|$  _______  $10$
SECTION C (15 Marks)(Long Answer Type - 5 Marks Each)
11.
(a) Arrange the following integers in descending order: $-12, 5, 0, -8, 15, -1$. (2 Marks)
(b) Simplify the expression: $[(-18) - (+5)] + [(-12) - (-20)]$. (3 Marks)
12.
Define the following geometrical terms clearly:
(a) Plane (2 Marks)
(b) Concurrent Lines (2 Marks)
(c) Point (1 Mark)
13.
(a) An airplane is flying at a height of $4500\text{ m}$ above sea level. Exactly below it, a submarine is floating $600\text{ m}$ below sea level. What is the vertical distance between them? Show the calculation using integers. (3 Marks)
(b) How many lines can pass through:
    (i) A single given point?
    (ii) Two distinct given points? (2 Marks)
