Ans 7.
Applying long division method to $5607$:
The nearest square is $74^2$ ($5476$).
Remainder when finding square root of 5607 is $5607 - 5476 = 131$. (1.5 Marks)
Least number to be subtracted $= 131$. (½ Mark)
Perfect Square $= 5607 - 131 = 5476$.
Square root of $5476$ is $74$. (1 Mark)
Ans 8.
Step-by-step long division of $12.0409$:
- Nearest square for $12$ is $3^2 = 9$ (Remainder = $3$). Bring down $04 \rightarrow 304$. (1 Mark)
- Divisor becomes $64 \times 4 = 256$ (Remainder = $48$). Bring down $09 \rightarrow 4809$. (1 Mark)
- Divisor becomes $687 \times 7 = 4809$ (Remainder = $0$).
Therefore, $\sqrt{12.0409} = 3.47$ (1 Mark)
Ans 9.
$x = \left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^2 \times \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{-4}$
Using $(a/b)^{-n} = (b/a)^n$: $x = \left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^2 \times \left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^4$ (1 Mark)
$x = \left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{2+4} = \left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^6$ (1 Mark)
Now find $x^{-2}$: $\left[ \left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^6 \right]^{-2} = \left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{-12} = \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{12}$ (1 Mark)
Ans 10.
Let the number of rows be $r$. Then, plants in each row = $r$.
Total plants $= r \times r = r^2$. (1 Mark)
Given $r^2 = 2025 \implies r = \sqrt{2025}$. (1 Mark)
Prime factorization or division shows $\sqrt{2025} = 45$.
Therefore, number of rows = 45, and number of plants in each row = 45. (1 Mark)
SECTION C (15 Marks)
Ans 11.
(a) Greatest 4-digit number is $9999$. (½ Mark)
Find the square root by long division: $99^2 = 9801$. Remainder = $198$. (1.5 Marks)
Subtract remainder from $9999$: $9999 - 198 = 9801$.
Greatest 4-digit perfect square is $9801$. (1 Mark)

(b) $\left(x^{a-b}\right)^{a+b} \times \left(x^{b-c}\right)^{b+c} \times \left(x^{c-a}\right)^{c+a}$ (1 Mark)
$= x^{a^2 - b^2} \times x^{b^2 - c^2} \times x^{c^2 - a^2}$
$= x^{(a^2 - b^2) + (b^2 - c^2) + (c^2 - a^2)} = x^0 = 1$ (1 Mark)
Ans 12.
(a) Prime factorization of $1323$: $1323 = 3 \times 3 \times 3 \times 7 \times 7$. (1 Mark)
The prime factor $3$ is left unpaired. Hence, the number must be divided by $3$. (1 Mark)
Quotient $= 1323 \div 3 = 441$.
Square root of the quotient $= \sqrt{441} = \sqrt{3^2 \times 7^2} = 3 \times 7 = 21$. (1 Mark)

(b) $\left( a^{-2-3} \cdot b^{3-(-2)} \right)^{-2}$ (1 Mark)
$= \left( a^{-5} \cdot b^5 \right)^{-2} = a^{10} \cdot b^{-10} = \frac{a^{10}}{b^{10}}$ (1 Mark)
Ans 13.
Let the side of the square field be $s$.
Area $= s^2 = 60025 \text{ m}^2 \implies s = \sqrt{60025}$. (1 Mark)
Using long division or prime factorization, $s = 245 \text{ m}$. (1 Mark)
Perimeter of the field (Distance to travel) $= 4 \times s = 4 \times 245 = 980 \text{ m}$. (1 Mark)
Speed $= 18 \text{ km/h} = 18 \times \frac{5}{18} \text{ m/s} = 5 \text{ m/s}$. (1 Mark)
Time taken $= \frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Speed}} = \frac{980}{5} = 196 \text{ seconds}$.
Time $= 3 \text{ minutes and } 16 \text{ seconds}$. (1 Mark)
