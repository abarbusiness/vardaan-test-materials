Rise in temperature = $+8^{\circ}\text{C}$ (½ Mark)
Morning temperature = Initial temperature + Rise in temperature
Morning temperature = $-5 + 8$ (1 Mark)
Morning temperature = $3^{\circ}\text{C}$ (1 Mark)

(a) Plane: A plane is a flat, two-dimensional surface that extends infinitely in all directions. It has length and breadth but no thickness. (2 Marks)

(b) Concurrent Lines: Three or more lines in a plane are said to be concurrent if they all intersect at the same single point. That common point is called the point of concurrence. (2 Marks)

Ans 13.
(a) Distance between Airplane and Submarine:
Height of airplane (above sea level) = $+4500\text{ m}$ (½ Mark)
Position of submarine (below sea level) = $-600\text{ m}$ (½ Mark)
Vertical distance between them = (Position of airplane) - (Position of submarine)
$= 4500 - (-600)$ (1 Mark)
$= 4500 + 600$
$= 5100\text{ m}$ (1 Mark)

(b) Number of Lines:
(i) An infinite number of lines can pass through a single given point. (1 Mark)
