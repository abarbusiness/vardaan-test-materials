Minor Test 8
Class: 6 ICSE Subject: Mathematics Time: 1.5 Hours Max. Marks: 50
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Working/calculations must be shown clearly alongside the answers.
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Objective / Short Answer Type - Attempt all questions - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Evaluate the following expression using the rules of integers:   \( -42 + (-35) - (-18) \)
2.
Convert the mixed fraction \( 5\frac{3}{4} \) into an improper fraction and write its reciprocal.
3.
Write three rational numbers equivalent to \( \frac{-3}{7} \).
4.
Arrange the following integers in ascending order:   \( 0, -15, 8, -4, 12, -1 \)
5.
Simplify the given fractions:   \( \frac{4}{9} + \frac{5}{12} \)
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - Attempt all questions - 3 Marks Each)
6.
A submarine is situated at 150 m below sea level. If it ascends 45 m, what is its new position? Express the initial, ascending, and final positions using appropriate integer signs.
7.
Simplify using BODMAS rule:   \( \frac{2}{3} \times \left( \frac{3}{4} - \frac{1}{8} \right) + \frac{1}{2} \)
8.
A ribbon of length \( 12\frac{1}{2} \) meters is cut into 5 equal pieces. Find the length of each piece.
9.
Subtract the sum of \( -250 \) and \( 138 \) from the sum of \( 136 \) and \( -272 \).
10.
Draw a number line and accurately represent the rational numbers \( \frac{3}{4} \) and \( \frac{-5}{4} \) on it.
SECTION C (25 Marks)(Long Answer Type - Attempt all questions - 5 Marks Each)
11.
(a) Find the absolute value of the expression: \( |-15| - |-8| + |-3| \). (2 Marks)
(b) Which rational number is greater: \( \frac{-4}{5} \) or \( \frac{-5}{7} \)? Show your working by equating the denominators. (3 Marks)
12.
In a class of 40 students, \( \frac{1}{5} \) of the total number of students like to study English, \( \frac{2}{5} \) of the total number like to study Mathematics, and the remaining students like to study Science.
(a) How many students like to study Science? (3 Marks)
(b) What fraction of the total number of students like to study Science? (2 Marks)
13.
Simplify the following rational expression completely:

\( \left[ \frac{-3}{5} \times \frac{10}{9} \right] - \left[ \frac{1}{2} \div \frac{-1}{4} \right] \)
14.
In Siachen, the temperature at 12:00 noon was \( 12^\circ\text{C} \) above zero. If it decreases at the rate of \( 2^\circ\text{C} \) per hour until midnight:
(a) At what time would the temperature be \( 8^\circ\text{C} \) below zero? (3 Marks)
(b) What would be the temperature at midnight (12:00 AM)? (2 Marks)
15.
Simplify the following expression involving nested brackets:

\( 2\frac{1}{3} - \left[ 1\frac{1}{2} + \left\{ \frac{3}{4} - \left( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} \right) \right\} \right] \)
