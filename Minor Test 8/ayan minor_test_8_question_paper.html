General Instructions:

SECTION A (10 Marks)(Objective / Very Short Answer Type - 2 Marks Each)
1.
(Chemistry) Define a 'Mixture'. Give one example of a heterogeneous mixture.
2.
(Mathematics) Write the numeral for "Seventy-three million, four hundred five thousand, two hundred ten" and insert commas correctly.
3.
(Chemistry) Write the correct chemical symbols for the following elements:
(a) Lead     (b) Silver     (c) Phosphorus     (d) Copper
4.
(Mathematics) Find the difference between the place value and the face value of the digit '7' in the number 8,74,512.
5.
(Chemistry) What is meant by 'Atomicity'? State the atomicity of Ozone (O3).
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
6.
(Chemistry) Classify the following substances into Elements, Compounds, and Mixtures:
Air, 24-carat Gold, Carbon dioxide, Blood, Iron, Pure Water.
7.
(Mathematics) Simplify using BODMAS rules:
50 − [ 20 + { 30 − ( 20 − 5 ) } ]
8.
(Chemistry) State any three fundamental differences between a compound and a mixture.
9.
(Mathematics) Write the corresponding Roman Numerals for the following Hindu-Arabic numbers:
(a) 94     (b) 487     (c) 1050
10.
(Mathematics) Form the greatest and the smallest 6-digit numbers using the digits 7, 0, 4, 9, 2, and 8 exactly once. Find the sum of the greatest and smallest numbers so formed.
SECTION C (25 Marks)(Long Answer Type - 5 Marks Each)
11.
(Chemistry)
(a) Name the appropriate separation technique used to separate the components of the following mixtures: (3 Marks)
    (i) Sand and water
    (ii) Iron filings and sulphur powder
    (iii) Salt from seawater
(b) Why is water (H2O) considered a compound and not a mixture? Give two scientific reasons. (2 Marks)
12.
(Mathematics)
(a) Estimate the product 5,478 × 362 by rounding off each number to its nearest hundred. (2 Marks)
(b) A factory manufactures 2,825 screws a day. How many total screws did it produce in the month of January 2024? (3 Marks)
13.
(Chemistry)
(a) What is a chemical formula? What two pieces of information does a chemical formula provide about a molecule? (2 Marks)
(b) Identify the constituent elements and state the number of atoms of each element present in one molecule of:
    (i) Sulphuric acid (H2SO4) (1.5 Marks)
    (ii) Table Sugar (C12H22O11) (1.5 Marks)
14.
(Mathematics)
(a) Insert commas suitably and write the number name for 87595762 according to the Indian System of Numeration. (2 Marks)
(b) In an election, the successful candidate registered 5,77,500 votes and his nearest rival secured 3,48,700 votes. By what margin did the successful candidate win the election? (2 Marks)
(c) Write the successor and predecessor of 10,00,000. (1 Mark)
15.
(Integrated Application)
(a) [Chemistry] Differentiate between Homogeneous and Heterogeneous mixtures, providing one example of each. (2.5 Marks)
(b) [Mathematics] A town newspaper is published every day. One copy of the newspaper has 12 pages. Every day 11,980 copies are printed. How many total pages are printed in a day? If each page requires 2 grams of ink, what is the total mass of ink required in kilograms for one day's printing? (2.5 Marks)
