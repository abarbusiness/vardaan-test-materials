Sol 2.
Mixed fraction: \( 5\frac{3}{4} \)
Improper fraction = \( \frac{5 \times 4 + 3}{4} = \frac{23}{4} \)
Reciprocal of \( \frac{23}{4} = \frac{4}{23} \)
Answer: Improper: \( \frac{23}{4} \), Reciprocal: \( \frac{4}{23} \)
Sol 3.
Equivalent rational numbers to \( \frac{-3}{7} \):
Multiply numerator and denominator by 2, 3, and 4:
\( \frac{-3 \times 2}{7 \times 2} = \frac{-6}{14} \)
\( \frac{-3 \times 3}{7 \times 3} = \frac{-9}{21} \)
\( \frac{-3 \times 4}{7 \times 4} = \frac{-12}{28} \)
Answer: \( \frac{-6}{14}, \frac{-9}{21}, \frac{-12}{28} \) (or any valid multiples)
Sol 4.

Comparing the integers, we place the largest negative numbers first, followed by zero, then positive numbers.
Answer: \( -15, -4, -1, 0, 8, 12 \)
Sol 5.
\( \frac{4}{9} + \frac{5}{12} \)

= \( \frac{4 \times 4}{9 \times 4} + \frac{5 \times 3}{12 \times 3} \)

Answer: Initial: \( -150 \) m, Ascent: \( +45 \) m, Final Position: \( -105 \) m (or 105 m below sea level).
Sol 7.
\( \frac{2}{3} \times \left( \frac{3}{4} - \frac{1}{8} \right) + \frac{1}{2} \)
Bracket first: \( \frac{3}{4} - \frac{1}{8} \) = \( \frac{6}{8} - \frac{1}{8} = \frac{5}{8} \)
Now multiply: \( \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{5}{8} = \frac{10}{24} = \frac{5}{12} \)
Finally add: \( \frac{5}{12} + \frac{1}{2} = \frac{5}{12} + \frac{6}{12} = \frac{11}{12} \)
Answer: \( \frac{11}{12} \)
Sol 8.
Total length = \( 12\frac{1}{2} \) m = \( \frac{25}{2} \) m
Number of equal pieces = 5
Length of each piece = \( \frac{25}{2} \div 5 \)
= \( \frac{25}{2} \times \frac{1}{5} \)
= \( \frac{5}{2} \) m = \( 2\frac{1}{2} \) m
Answer: \( 2\frac{1}{2} \) m (or 2.5 m)

Sol 10.
Represent \( \frac{3}{4} \) and \( \frac{-5}{4} \) on a number line.
- Draw a straight horizontal line and mark 0 in the center.
- Divide the gap between each integer (0 to 1, 1 to 2, 0 to -1, -1 to -2) into 4 equal parts because the denominator is 4.
- To mark \( \frac{3}{4} \), move 3 parts to the right of 0.
- To mark \( \frac{-5}{4} \) (which is \( -1\frac{1}{4} \)), move 5 parts to the left of 0.
Answer: Check student's drawn number line for correct divisions and placements.
SECTION C (25 Marks)
Sol 11.
(a) \( |-15| - |-8| + |-3| \)
= \( 15 - 8 + 3 \)
= \( 7 + 3 = 10 \)

(b) Compare \( \frac{-4}{5} \) and \( \frac{-5}{7} \)
LCM of 5 and 7 is 35.
\( \frac{-4 \times 7}{5 \times 7} = \frac{-28}{35} \)
\( \frac{-5 \times 5}{7 \times 5} = \frac{-25}{35} \)
Since \( -25 > -28 \), \( \frac{-25}{35} > \frac{-28}{35} \).
Answer: (a) \( 10 \),   (b) \( \frac{-5}{7} \) is greater.
Sol 12.
Total students = 40.
(a) English = \( \frac{1}{5} \times 40 = 8 \) students.
Maths = \( \frac{2}{5} \times 40 = 16 \) students.
Science = Total - (English + Maths) = \( 40 - (8 + 16) = 40 - 24 = 16 \) students.

(b) Fraction for Science = \( \frac{\text{Science students}}{\text{Total students}} = \frac{16}{40} \)
Simplified = \( \frac{2}{5} \).
Answer: (a) \( 16 \) students,   (b) \( \frac{2}{5} \)
Sol 13.
\( \left[ \frac{-3}{5} \times \frac{10}{9} \right] - \left[ \frac{1}{2} \div \frac{-1}{4} \right] \)
First Bracket: \( \frac{-3}{5} \times \frac{10}{9} = \frac{-1}{1} \times \frac{2}{3} = \frac{-2}{3} \)
Second Bracket: \( \frac{1}{2} \div \frac{-1}{4} = \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{-4}{1} = -2 \)
Expression: \( \frac{-2}{3} - (-2) \)
= \( \frac{-2}{3} + 2 \)
= \( \frac{-2 + 6}{3} = \frac{4}{3} \) or \( 1\frac{1}{3} \)
Answer: \( \frac{4}{3} \) or \( 1\frac{1}{3} \)
Sol 14.
Initial Temp (12:00 noon) = \( 12^\circ\text{C} \). Rate of decrease = \( 2^\circ\text{C} \) per hour.
(a) Target Temp = \( -8^\circ\text{C} \).
Total decrease required = \( 12 - (-8) = 20^\circ\text{C} \).
Time taken = \( \frac{20}{2} = 10 \) hours.
10 hours after 12:00 noon is 10:00 PM.

(b) Midnight is 12 hours after 12:00 noon.
Total decrease in 12 hours = \( 12 \times 2 = 24^\circ\text{C} \).
Temperature at midnight = \( 12^\circ\text{C} - 24^\circ\text{C} = -12^\circ\text{C} \).
Answer: (a) 10:00 PM,   (b) \( -12^\circ\text{C} \)
Sol 15.
\( 2\frac{1}{3} - \left[ 1\frac{1}{2} + \left\{ \frac{3}{4} - \left( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} \right) \right\} \right] \)
Convert to improper: \( \frac{7}{3} - \left[ \frac{3}{2} + \left\{ \frac{3}{4} - \left( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} \right) \right\} \right] \)

Solve innermost bracket (): \( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} = \frac{3}{6} + \frac{2}{6} = \frac{5}{6} \)
Solve curly bracket {}: \( \frac{3}{4} - \frac{5}{6} \)   (LCM of 4, 6 is 12)
  = \( \frac{9}{12} - \frac{10}{12} = \frac{-1}{12} \)
Solve square bracket []: \( \frac{3}{2} + \left( \frac{-1}{12} \right) \)
  = \( \frac{18}{12} - \frac{1}{12} = \frac{17}{12} \)
Final step: \( \frac{7}{3} - \frac{17}{12} \)
  = \( \frac{28}{12} - \frac{17}{12} = \frac{11}{12} \)
Answer: \( \frac{11}{12} \)
