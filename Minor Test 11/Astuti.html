MINOR TEST 11
Class: 6 (ICSE) Subject: Mathematics Time: 1.5 Hours Max. Marks: 50
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly. Show working for all numericals.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Attempt all questions - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Evaluate the following using the rules of integers:
(a)
\( (-28) - (-14) \)
(b)
\( |-35| + |-12| \)
2.
Convert the mixed fraction \( 4 \frac{2}{5} \) into an improper fraction and then find its reciprocal.
3.
The temperature of a freezer was \( -6^\circ\text{C} \). If the temperature is further lowered by \( 5^\circ\text{C} \), what is the new temperature of the freezer?
4.
Find the integer which is \( 5 \) less than \( -3 \) and write its additive inverse.
5.
Add the following fractions: \( \frac{5}{12} \) and \( \frac{7}{18} \).
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Simplify the following expression using integer rules:
\( (-22) + (+35) - (-18) + (-11) \)
7.
Simplify the following fractions:
\( \frac{4}{5} + \frac{7}{10} - \frac{1}{2} \)
8.
Out of a total of 60 students in a class, \( \frac{3}{5} \) like playing cricket, \( \frac{1}{4} \) like playing football, and the remaining students like playing tennis. Find the total number of students who like playing tennis.
9.
Subtract the sum of \( -250 \) and \( 138 \) from the sum of \( 136 \) and \( -272 \).
10.
An elevator descends into a mine shaft at the rate of \( 6 \text{ m/min} \). If the descent starts from \( 10 \text{ m} \) above the ground level, how long will it take to reach a depth of \( -350 \text{ m} \)?
SECTION C (25 Marks)(Long Answer Type - 5 Marks Each)
11.
Simplify using the BODMAS rule:
\( 3 \frac{1}{5} - \left[ 2 \frac{1}{2} - \left\{ 1 \frac{1}{3} - \left( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{6} \right) \right\} \right] \)
12.
A man earns ₹45,000 per month. He spends \( \frac{1}{3} \) of his income on rent, \( \frac{1}{5} \) on food, and \( \frac{1}{6} \) on the education of his children. Calculate:
(a)
The total amount of money spent by him. (2 Marks)
(b)
The amount of money he saves every month. (2 Marks)
(c)
The fraction of his total income that he saves. (1 Mark)
13.
(a)
Arrange the following integers in ascending order: \( -15, 23, -8, 0, -2, 12 \). (2 Marks)
(b)
Using a number line concept (or stepwise calculation showing direction), evaluate: \( (-4) + 6 + (-3) \). Explain your steps briefly. (3 Marks)
14.
Ramesh reads \( \frac{1}{5} \) part of a book in 1 hour.
(a)
How much part of the book will he read in \( 2 \frac{1}{5} \) hours? (2 Marks)
(b)
If the book contains a total of 250 pages, how many pages will he read in that time? (3 Marks)
15.
A deep-sea diver is initially \( 20 \text{ m} \) below sea level. He then goes down further by \( 15 \text{ m} \), and finally rises by \( 8 \text{ m} \).
(a)
Determine his final position relative to the sea level using integers. (2 Marks)
(b)
If a bird is flying exactly \( 35 \text{ m} \) above sea level directly above the diver, what is the total vertical distance between the bird and the diver at his final position? (3 Marks)
