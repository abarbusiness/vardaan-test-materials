3.

SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
6.

7.

8.

9.

10.
A storybook contains 120 pages. Aryan read \( \frac{3}{5} \) of the book on Sunday. How many pages are still left to be read? [3]
SECTION C (25 Marks)(Long Answer Type - 5 Marks Each)
11.
Simplify the following expression: \[ 5 \frac{1}{2} - \left[ 2 \frac{1}{3} \div \left\{ \frac{3}{4} - \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{6} \right) \right\} \right] \] [5]
12.
An elevator descends into a mine shaft at the rate of \( 6 \text{ m/min} \). If the descent starts from \( 10 \text{ m} \) above the ground level, how long will it take for the elevator to reach \( -350 \text{ m} \)? [5]
13.
In a school, \( \frac{4}{9} \) of the total students are boys. If the number of girls is \( 775 \), find:
(i)
The total number of students in the school.
(ii)
The number of boys in the school.
[5]
14.
Simplify by applying integer rules properly: \[ (-20) + (-8) \div (-2) \times 3 - [ 5 \times (-2) - (-3) \times (-4) ] \] [5]
15.
