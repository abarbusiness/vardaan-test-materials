Minor Test 01 - Surprise Test
Class: 10 CBSE (Bridge Test) Subject: Mathematics (Algebra & Prime Factorization) Time: 1 Hour Max. Marks: 40
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Section A comprises 5 questions of 2 marks each.
  • Section B comprises 5 questions of 3 marks each.
  • Section C comprises 3 questions of 5 marks each.
  • Write your answers neatly, showing all necessary calculation steps clearly.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Objective / Short Answer Type - Attempt all questions - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Find the value of the polynomial p(x) = 5x − 4x2 + 3 at x = 0 and x = −1.
2.
Write any two valid solutions for the linear equation 2x + y = 7.
3.
Find the Highest Common Factor (HCF) and Least Common Multiple (LCM) of 12, 15, and 21 using the prime factorization method.
4.
Write the exact coordinates of the origin. Also, specify the quadrant in which the point (−3, 4) lies.
5.
If two interior angles on the same side of a transversal intersecting two parallel lines are in the ratio 2:3, find the measure of the greater angle.
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - Attempt all questions - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Evaluate 992 without direct multiplication, by using a suitable algebraic identity.
7.
Use the Factor Theorem to mathematically determine whether (x + 1) is a factor of the polynomial x3 + x2 + x + 1.
8.
Find the HCF of 96 and 404 by the prime factorization method. Hence, find their LCM using the fundamental property of two numbers.
9.
The cost of a notebook is twice the cost of a pen. Write a linear equation in two variables (x and y) to represent this algebraic statement.
10.
In an isosceles triangle ABC with AB = AC, if ∠B = 50°, find the measures of ∠A and ∠C. Provide the geometric reasoning.
SECTION C (15 Marks)(Long Answer Type - Attempt all questions - 5 Marks Each)
11.
Factorise the given cubic polynomial completely:
x3 − 2x2 − x + 2
12.
Draw the graph of the linear equation x + y = 4 on a Cartesian plane. From your plotted graph, find the coordinates of the exact points where the line intersects the x-axis and the y-axis.
13.
Three bells toll at intervals of 12 minutes, 15 minutes, and 18 minutes respectively. If they start tolling together at 9:00 AM, at what exact time will they next toll together? Use the prime factorization method to logically determine the answer, showing all steps.
