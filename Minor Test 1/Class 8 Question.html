MINOR TEST 01 (SURPRISE TEST)
Class: 8 CBSE Subject: Basic Mathematics Time: 1 Hour Max. Marks: 40
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Show all working steps clearly. Marks will be deducted for direct answers without calculation.
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Attempt all questions - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Find the Highest Common Factor (HCF) and Least Common Multiple (LCM) of 24 and 36.
2.
Simplify the following expression: 3/4 + 5/6 - 1/3
3.
Evaluate using integer rules: (-15) × 4 + 25 ÷ (-5)
4.
Simplify the algebraic expression by combining like terms: 3x - 5y + 2x + 7y
5.
Find the prime factorization of 1024 and express it in exponential form.
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Three bells toll at intervals of 12, 15, and 20 minutes respectively. If they all toll together at 8:00 AM, at what time will they toll together again?
7.
A man spends 2/5 of his monthly salary on food and 3/10 on house rent. If he still has ₹3000 left with him, calculate his total monthly salary.
8.
Find three rational numbers between -1/2 and 1/4.
9.
The cost of 15 identical notebooks is ₹187.50. Using the unitary method, find the cost of 8 such notebooks.
10.
The length of a rectangular park is 15 1/2 m and its breadth is 10 2/3 m. Find the perimeter of the park.
SECTION C (15 Marks)(Long Answer Type - 5 Marks Each)
11.
In a school, 4/9 of the total students are girls. If there are 360 boys in the school, find:
(a) The total number of students in the school. (2 Marks)
(b) The number of girls in the school. (1.5 Marks)
(c) The ratio of the number of boys to the number of girls in its simplest form. (1.5 Marks)
12.
Find the greatest 4-digit number which is exactly divisible by 12, 18, 24, and 32. Show all the steps of division and LCM calculation clearly.
13.
Ram bought 3 1/2 kg of apples at ₹80 per kg and 2 3/4 kg of grapes at ₹120 per kg.
(a) How much money did he spend on apples? (1.5 Marks)
(b) How much money did he spend on grapes? (1.5 Marks)
(c) If he handed a ₹1000 note to the shopkeeper, how much change will he get back? (2 Marks)
