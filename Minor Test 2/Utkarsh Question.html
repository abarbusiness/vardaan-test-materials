Minor Test
Class: 9 CBSE Subject: Science & Mathematics Time: 1.5 Hours Max. Marks: 60
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Section A comprises 6 questions of 2 marks each.
  • Section B comprises 6 questions of 3 marks each.
  • Section C comprises 6 questions of 5 marks each.
  • Write your answers neatly and draw diagrams/graphs where required.
SECTION A (12 Marks)(Attempt all questions - 2 Marks Each)
Part I: Science (Matter and Pure Substances)
1.
Define matter. Name the three fundamental states of matter.
2.
Write any two key differences between a pure substance and a mixture.
3.
Why does a desert cooler cool better on a hot, dry day?
Part II: Mathematics
4.
Simplify the following expression: $(3 + \sqrt{3})(3 - \sqrt{3})$.
5.
Find the value of the polynomial $P(x) = 5x - 4x^2 + 3$ at $x = 2$.
6.
Express the linear equation $2x + 3y = 9.3\overline{5}$ in the standard form $ax + by + c = 0$ and indicate the values of $a, b,$ and $c$.
SECTION B (18 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
Part I: Science (Matter and Pure Substances)
7.
State any three characteristics of the particles of matter.
8.
Define the following terms clearly:
(a) Melting point
(b) Boiling point
(c) Latent heat of fusion
9.

Part II: Mathematics
10.

11.

12.

SECTION C (30 Marks)(Long Answer Type - 5 Marks Each)
Part I: Science (Matter and Pure Substances)
13.

(a) Shape
(b) Volume
(c) Compressibility
(d) Intermolecular space
(e) Kinetic energy of particles

15.

(b) List and briefly explain any three factors that affect the rate of evaporation of a liquid. (3 Marks)
Part II: Mathematics
16.
Apply the concepts of Number Systems to solve the following:
(a) Rationalize the denominator of $\frac{1}{7 + 3\sqrt{2}}$. (3 Marks)

17.

(a) Factorize the cubic polynomial completely: $x^3 - 2x^2 - x + 2$. (3 Marks)
(b) Evaluate the following using a suitable algebraic identity: $(99)^3$. (2 Marks)
18.
Linear equations geometry:
