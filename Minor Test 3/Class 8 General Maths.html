MINOR TEST 03
Class: 8 Subject: Mathematics Time: 1 Hour Max. Marks: 50
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly. Show all working steps clearly.
  • Use of calculators is strictly prohibited.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Attempt all questions - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Using the laws of exponents, simplify the following expression:
(23 × 34 × 4) / (3 × 32)
2.
Find three rational numbers between 1/4 and 1/2.
3.
Solve the following linear equation for x:
3(x - 3) = 5(2x + 1)
4.
If the measure of an angle is 45°, what are the measures of its complementary and supplementary angles?
5.
A ratio is given as 3:4. Convert this ratio into a percentage.
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Simplify the algebraic expression:
4x2 - 3y2 + 5xy - (2x2 - 5y2 + xy)
Also, evaluate the simplified expression if x = 1 and y = -1.
7.
A shopkeeper bought a chair for ₹375 and sold it for ₹400. Find his profit or loss percentage.
8.
The sum of three consecutive odd integers is 69. Find the integers.
9.
In a right-angled triangle, if the length of the hypotenuse is 13 cm and one of the other sides is 5 cm, find the length of the third side.
10.
Express the distance from the Earth to the Moon, which is approximately 384,000,000 meters, in standard scientific notation.
SECTION C (25 Marks)(Long Answer Type - 5 Marks Each)
11.
The denominator of a rational number is greater than its numerator by 8. If the numerator is increased by 17 and the denominator is decreased by 1, the number obtained is 3/2. Find the original rational number.
12.
Use appropriate algebraic identities to evaluate the following:
(a) 103 × 104
(b) 992
13.
A rectangular garden is 90 m long and 75 m broad. A path 5 m wide is to be built outside and completely surrounding it. Find the area of the path in square meters. Also, find the area of the garden in hectares.
14.
Solve the given equation for x using the laws of exponents:
52x+1 ÷ 25 = 125
15.
The population of a city was 20,000 in the year 1997. It increased at the compound rate of 5% per annum. Find the population at the end of the year 2000.
