SECTION A (15 Marks)(Attempt all questions - 1 Mark Each)
1.
Fill in the blanks with appropriate biological terms:
(a) The system of giving scientific names consisting of two words is called _________ nomenclature.
(b) Animals with jointed appendages (legs) belong to the phylum _________.
(c) _________ are known as the amphibians of the plant kingdom.
(d) Plants bearing naked seeds are called _________.
(e) The five-kingdom classification system was proposed by _________.
2.
State whether the following statements are True or False. If False, rewrite the statement correctly by changing the underlined word(s):
(a) Aves are cold-blooded animals.
(b) Monocot plants have leaves with reticulate venation.
(c) Sponges belong to the phylum Coelenterata.
(d) Fungi are autotrophic organisms.
(e) The body of a flatworm is dorsiventrally flattened.
3.
Give one word or a brief biological term for the following:
(a) The largest phylum of the animal kingdom.
(b) The kingdom containing unicellular prokaryotic organisms like bacteria.
(c) Animals having a backbone or vertebral column.
(d) An undifferentiated plant body without true roots, stem, and leaves.
(e) The numerous tiny pores present on the body of sponges (Porifera).
