PART III: GENERAL MATHEMATICS

31. (B) 60: Multiples of 12: 12, 24, 36, 48, 60... Multiples of 15: 15, 30, 45, 60... Multiples of 20: 20, 40, 60... The lowest common multiple is 60.
32. (A) 300: Let the number be x. Given, 40% of x = 120. So, (40/100) × x = 120. x = (120 × 100) / 40 = 300.
33. (A) 3.0: 2.5 × 1.2 = 3.00. Therefore, the value is 3.0.
34. (A) 19: If x + 5 = 12, then x = 7. Substitute x = 7 into the expression 3x - 2: 3(7) - 2 = 21 - 2 = 19.
35. (B) 12: The ratio is 3:2. Total parts = 3 + 2 = 5. Total students = 30. Value of 1 part = 30 / 5 = 6. Number of girls = 2 parts = 2 × 6 = 12.
36. (A) 120 cm²: Area of rectangle = Length × Width = 15 cm × 8 cm = 120 cm².
37. (B) ₹ 100: Simple Interest = (P × R × T) / 100 = (1000 × 5 × 2) / 100 = 10000 / 100 = 100.
38. (C) 5: "Subtract -8 from -3" is written mathematically as: -3 - (-8) = -3 + 8 = 5.
39. (B) 180°: The sum of all interior angles of any triangle is always 180°.
40. (C) 72: Calculate the exponents first: 2³ = 8 and 3² = 9. Then multiply: 8 × 9 = 72.

PART IV: APTITUDE & REASONING

41. (B) 63: The logic follows a pattern of multiplying by 2 and adding 1. (3×2)+1=7, (7×2)+1=15, (15×2)+1=31. Therefore, next is (31×2)+1=63. (Alternatively: differences are +4, +8, +16, +32).
42. (A) FQI: Each letter in CAT is moved forward by +2 positions in the alphabet. C (+2) = E; A (+2) = C; T (+2) = V. Applying to DOG: D (+2) = F; O (+2) = Q; G (+2) = I.
43. (C) Sister: "My grandfather's only son" refers to the man's own father. The "daughter of my father" is his sister.
44. (B) South: He walks 5 km East, turns right (facing South) and walks 3 km, turns right again (facing West) and walks 5 km. He is exactly 3 km due South of his starting position.
45. (B) Pressure: A thermometer is an instrument used to measure temperature. Similarly, a barometer is an instrument used to measure atmospheric pressure.
46. (A) 100: The symbol '@' denotes the sum of the squares of the two numbers. 3² + 4² = 9 + 16 = 25. 5² + 12² = 25 + 144 = 169. Therefore, 6² + 8² = 36 + 64 = 100.
47. (A) 1, 3, 4, 2: The logical order of a plant's life cycle is: Seed (1) → Plant (3) → Flower (4) → Fruit (2).
48. (C) O: The gap between the letters increases progressively. A (+2) = C; C (+3) = F; F (+4) = J. Therefore, J (+5) = O.
49. (A) 15: The monkey has a net gain of 1m every 2 minutes (climbs 3, slips 2). In 14 minutes, it reaches 7 meters. In the 15th minute, it climbs 3 meters and reaches the top (10 meters). Once at the top, it won't slip down.
50. (B) B: Let's arrange them. 'A is right of B' ➔ B A. 'E is right of A' ➔ B A E. 'C is left of B' ➔ C B A E. 'C is right of D' ➔ D C B A E. The exact middle person of the five is B.
