22.
Ans:
Cost Price (C.P.) of washing machine = Rs. 12,000.
Desired Profit = 20% of C.P. = (20/100) × 12000 = Rs. 2400. [1.5 Marks]
Selling Price (S.P.) = C.P. + Profit = 12000 + 2400 = Rs. 14,400. [1 Mark]
Let the Marked Price (M.P.) be Rs. x.
Discount = 20% of x = 0.2x.
S.P. = M.P. - Discount ⇒ 14400 = x - 0.2x ⇒ 14400 = 0.8x. [1.5 Marks]
x = 14400 / 0.8 = Rs. 18,000.
Therefore, Marked Price is Rs. 18,000. [1 Mark]

Ans:
For First Bicycle:
C.P. = Rs. 3000, Profit = 10%.
Profit amount = 10% of 3000 = Rs. 300.
S.P.₁ = 3000 + 300 = Rs. 3300. [1.5 Marks]

For Second Bicycle:
C.P. = Rs. 3000, Loss = 10%.
Loss amount = 10% of 3000 = Rs. 300.
S.P.₂ = 3000 - 300 = Rs. 2700. [1.5 Marks]

Total Transaction:
Total C.P. = 3000 + 3000 = Rs. 6000.
Total S.P. = 3300 + 2700 = Rs. 6000. [1 Mark]
Since Total S.P. = Total C.P., there is neither profit nor loss (0%). [1 Mark]
