Apply multiplication law in numerator: 72n+3 [1.5 Marks]
Apply division law: 7(2n+3) - 4 = 75
72n - 1 = 75 [1.5 Marks]
Since the bases are equal, their exponents must be equal:
2n - 1 = 5 [1 Mark]
2n = 5 + 1 ⇒ 2n = 6
n = 6 / 2 ⇒ n = 3 [1 Mark]
