SECTION B (15 Marks)(Formal Letter Writing)
11.
Attempt ANY ONE of the following:

A. Letter of Complaint: You are Ankit/Ankita, residing at 45, Civil Lines, Kanpur. Last week, you purchased an "Eco-Wash" automatic washing machine from M/s Electronic World, Mall Road. However, the machine has developed several faults; it makes a loud noise while spinning and the dryer is not functioning. Write a letter of complaint in 120-150 words to the Sales Manager, requesting an immediate replacement or repair under warranty.
OR
B. Letter to Editor: You are Rohan/Rohini, a resident of Sector-4, Dwarka, New Delhi. You are deeply concerned about the pathetic condition of roads and several open, uncovered manholes in your locality, which have caused multiple accidents recently, especially at night. Write a letter to the Editor of 'The National Times' in 120-150 words, drawing the attention of the civic authorities to this grave issue and suggesting immediate safety measures.
