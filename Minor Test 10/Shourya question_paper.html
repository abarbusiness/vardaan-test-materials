SECTION B: LITERATURE (20 Marks)
4.
Read the extract given below and answer the questions that follow: [10 Marks]
"The way a crow
Shook down on me
The dust of snow
From a hemlock tree"
(a)
Name the poem and the poet. [2]
(b)
Where was the poet at this moment and what was his state of mind? [2]
(c)
What exactly is the "dust of snow"? How did it fall on the poet? [2]
(d)
What do the 'crow' and the 'hemlock tree' symbolize in this stanza? Why has the poet chosen them? [4]
5.
Answer the following questions in 40-50 words based on the poem "Dust of Snow": [10 Marks]
(a)
How does the falling of the dust of snow bring about a change in the poet's mood? [4]
(b)
The poem conveys a deep philosophical message using simple incidents of nature. Elaborate on the central theme of the poem. [6]
