Answer Key & Marking Scheme - Minor Test 10 (Class 6)
SECTION A (10 Marks)
Ans 1.
The Taj Mahal is situated on the banks of the river Yamuna in Agra.
Marking: 0.5 marks for capitalizing 'The', 'Taj Mahal', 'Yamuna', 'Agra' correctly. 0.5 marks for the full stop. (Deduct 0.5 for each missing capitalization/punctuation, up to 2 marks).
Ans 2.
Prepositions: over, into
Marking: 1 mark each for identifying 'over' and 'into'.
Ans 3.
Rohan was very tired, but / yet he continued to study for his exams.
(OR: Although Rohan was very tired, he continued to study for his exams.)
Marking: 2 marks for a grammatically correct sentence using a valid conjunction.
Ans 4.
She is sitting in the shade of a large oak tree in the garden.
Marking: 1 mark for each correct preposition.
Ans 5.
Alas! We have lost the final match by one goal.
Marking: 1 mark for 'Alas!' (Capital A and exclamation). 1 mark for capitalizing 'We' and the full stop.
SECTION B (20 Marks)

Marking: 4 marks overall. Deduct 0.5 marks for every punctuation/capitalization error. Key checkpoints: Capital 'One', comma after day (optional), full stop after forest, Capital 'He', full stop after beak, Capital 'The', comma after said, opening quotes, capital 'Oh', closing quotes.

Ans 10.
