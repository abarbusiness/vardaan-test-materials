ANSWER KEY & MARKING SCHEME - MINOR TEST
SECTION A (10 Marks)
Ans 1.
The prizes were distributed to the winners by the chief guest. [2M]
Rule: Object 'the prizes' becomes the subject. Past tense 'distributed' changes to 'were distributed'.
Ans 2.
The principal announced the new policy yesterday. [2M]
Rule: Subject 'the principal' takes the active position. 'was announced' reverts to simple past 'announced'.
Ans 3.
The teacher told the students that the Earth revolves around the Sun. [2M]
Rule: Universal truths do not change tense in reported speech. 'Said to' becomes 'told'.
Ans 4.
He said to me, "Please wait here till I return." [2M]
Rule: 'Requested' indicates the use of 'Please'. 'There' changes back to 'here'. 'He returned' (past) changes to 'I return' (present).
