General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A (Grammar), B (Applied Grammar), and C (Literature).
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly. Adhere strictly to word limits where applicable.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Grammar: Tense Identification & Application - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Fill in the blank with the correct form of the verb given in brackets and explicitly name the tense you have used:
By the time the chief guest arrived, the students ________ (complete) their cultural performance.
2.
Fill in the blank with the correct form of the verb given in brackets and explicitly name the tense you have used:
I ________ (work) on this science project since last Monday and it is still incomplete.
3.
Fill in the blank with the correct form of the verb given in brackets and explicitly name the tense you have used:
The Prime Minister ________ (leave) for Japan next week to attend the summit.
4.
Fill in the blank with the correct form of the verb given in brackets and explicitly name the tense you have used:
While my mother was reading a book, my younger brother ________ (play) loud music in the living room.
5.
Fill in the blank with the correct form of the verb given in brackets and explicitly name the tense you have used:
Water ________ (boil) at 100 degrees Celsius under standard atmospheric pressure.
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Grammar: Error Correction & Transformation - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Identify the error in the following sentence, provide the correction, and state the grammatical rule regarding the tense used:
"He didn't went to the market to buy groceries yesterday."
7.
Rewrite the following sentence into the Future Perfect Continuous Tense. (Assume the action continues into a specific future time).
"The farmers are ploughing the fields."
8.
Identify the error in the following sentence and rewrite it correctly. Why is the continuous form incorrect here?
"I am knowing the answer to this complex mathematical problem."
9.
Rewrite the following sentence into the Present Perfect Tense and explain how the meaning of the sentence changes compared to the original.
"She writes a letter to the principal."
10.
Identify the error in the sequence of tenses in the following sentence and correct it:
"The train left the station before we reached the platform."
