Minor Test 05: English Grammar (Set B)
Class: 6 & 7 Subject: English (Tenses) Time: 1 Hour Max. Marks: 50
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Objective Type - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Fill in the blank with the Simple Present form of the verb:
My father ________ (drink) coffee every morning.
2.
Fill in the blank with the Simple Past form of the verb:
We ________ (watch) a great movie last night.
3.
Fill in the blank with the Simple Future form of the verb:
I think it ________ (rain) tomorrow.
4.
Name the tense used in the following sentence:
"The boys are playing football in the park."
5.
Change the following sentence into Simple Past tense:
"She sings a beautiful song."
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer Type - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Choose the correct verb to complete the sentence (Subject-Verb Agreement):
(a) The dog ________ (bark/barks) loudly.
(b) They ________ (go/goes) to school by bus.
(c) Rahul and Amit ________ (is/are) good friends.
7.
Rewrite the sentences using the Present Continuous Tense:
(a) I eat an apple.
(b) The birds fly in the sky.
8.
Change these positive sentences into negative sentences in the Simple Past tense:
(a) He bought a new pen.
(b) They played well.
9.
Match the sentence to its correct tense:
1. She will dance.       (a) Simple Past
2. He wrote a letter.    (b) Simple Future
3. I am reading.        (c) Present Continuous
10.
Write one sentence about what you do every day (Simple Present) and one sentence about what you did yesterday (Simple Past).
SECTION C (25 Marks)(Long Answer/Application - 5 Marks Each)
11.
Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verbs given in brackets. Read carefully to understand the time!
Every day, Rahul ________ (wake) up early. Yesterday, he ________ (wake) up late because he ________ (be) tired. Tomorrow, he ________ (set) an alarm so that he ________ (not miss) the bus.
12.
Rewrite the following paragraph by changing the verbs from Present Tense to Past Tense:
"The teacher enters the class. She writes on the blackboard. The students listen carefully. They open their books and start reading."
13.
Fill in the blanks with the Past Continuous form of the verbs (was/were + -ing):
It was 7 PM. Mother ________ (cook) dinner in the kitchen. My sister ________ (watch) television. I ________ (do) my homework. My father and uncle ________ (talk) in the living room. The dog ________ (sleep) on the mat.
14.
Make meaningful sentences using the following verbs in the tenses mentioned in the brackets:
(a) Play (Simple Past)
(b) Read (Present Continuous)
(c) Visit (Simple Future)
(d) Jump (Simple Present)
(e) Eat (Past Continuous)
15.
Spot the error! Underline the wrong word in each sentence and write the correct word next to it:
(a) He go to the park yesterday.
(b) I is writing a letter right now.
(c) They will goes to the zoo tomorrow.
(d) She did not finished her work.
(e) We was very happy yesterday.
