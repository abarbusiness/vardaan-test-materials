Minor Test 05
Class: 9 Subject: English (Topic: Tense) Time: 1 Hour Max. Marks: 50
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly. Pay attention to grammatical accuracy.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Objective/Short Answer - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Fill in the blanks with the correct past tense (Simple Past or Past Continuous):
When the teacher ________ (enter) the classroom, the students ________ (make) a lot of noise.
2.
Identify the tense in the following sentence and convert it to Present Perfect Tense:
"She writes a letter to her grandmother every week."
3.
Fill in the blank using the Future Continuous tense and state the general rule for forming this tense:
At this time tomorrow, I ________ (travel) to New Delhi.
4.
Choose the correct option and briefly justify your choice:
He ________ (has gone / went) to the market yesterday to buy some fresh vegetables.
5.
Define the 'Present Perfect Continuous' tense. Provide one original example sentence to illustrate its use.
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer/Reasoning - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Identify the error in the following sentence, provide the correction, and explain the grammatical rule violated:
"I am having this car since 2020."
7.
Analyze the sequence of tenses in the following sentence. Why are two different past tenses used here?
"The train had already left the station before we reached the platform."
8.
Fill in the blanks with the appropriate tenses to show correct conditional future relation:
By the time you ________ (receive) this parcel, I ________ (leave) for London.
9.
Rewrite the following sentence correctly. Explain why the continuous form is incorrect in this context.
"Pure water is boiling at 100 degrees Celsius."
10.
Differentiate between the 'Simple Past' and 'Present Perfect' tense. Support your answer by writing two sentences that highlight this difference.
SECTION C (25 Marks)(Long Answer/Complex Application - 5 Marks Each)
11.

Neha: I ________ (submit) it by next Friday.
Aman: Really? By Friday, I ________ (still/work) on my conclusion.
Neha: Don't worry, by the time the deadline arrives, we both ________ (finish) our work.
Aman: I hope so! I ________ (start) writing the final draft tomorrow.
13.
Transformation Challenge: Transform the following chronological passage from Simple Present tense to Past Perfect tense, assuming all actions were completed before another major event in the past. (Make minor structural adjustments if necessary).
"He wakes up early in the morning. He packs his heavy luggage. He locks all the doors. He leaves for the international airport."
