Example: He had slept before I reached his house. (1 mark for definition + 1 mark for correct example)

6.
Error: since ten years
Correction: They have been living in this city for ten years.
Rule: 'For' is used to denote a duration/period of time, whereas 'since' is used for a specific point in time in the past. (1m error + 1m correction + 1m rule)
