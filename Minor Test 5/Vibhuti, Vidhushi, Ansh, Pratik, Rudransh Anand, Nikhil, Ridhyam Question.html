Minor Test 05: English Grammar (Set A)
Class: 8 Subject: English (Tenses) Time: 1 Hour Max. Marks: 50
General Instructions:
  • All questions are compulsory.
  • The paper is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.
  • Write your answers neatly and legibly. Check your spellings.
SECTION A (10 Marks)(Objective/Short Answer - 2 Marks Each)
1.
Fill in the blanks with the correct form of verbs (Past Continuous or Simple Past):
While the children ________ (play) in the garden, it suddenly ________ (start) to rain.
2.
Identify the tense of the given sentence and convert it into Present Perfect Tense:
"The mechanic is repairing the car."
3.
Fill in the blank using the Future Perfect tense:
By the end of this month, our school ________ (complete) the new library building.
4.
Choose the correct option:
She (has been studying / is studying) French since morning and looks very tired now.
5.
Define the 'Past Perfect' tense. Write one sentence as an example.
SECTION B (15 Marks)(Short Answer/Reasoning - 3 Marks Each)
6.
Identify the error in the use of 'since' or 'for', correct the sentence, and explain the rule:
"They have been living in this city since ten years."
7.
Combine the following two sentences using the Past Perfect and Simple Past tenses appropriately:
"The patient died. The doctor arrived."
8.
Transform the following sentence into Negative and Interrogative forms of the same tense:
"He had submitted his assignment."
9.
Explain the difference in meaning between these two sentences:
(A) Water boils at 100°C.
(B) The water is boiling; please turn off the stove.
10.
Fill in the blanks to complete the conditional sentence:
If you ________ (study) hard, you ________ (pass) the examination easily.
SECTION C (25 Marks)(Long Answer/Complex Application - 5 Marks Each)
11.
Paragraph Editing: The following paragraph has one verb-related error in each line. Identify the incorrect word and supply the correct word. Write the incorrect and correct words in a format (a) to (e).
Yesterday, I go to the market to buy groceries. (a)
While I was walked down the street, (b)
I see a cute little puppy near a shop. (c)
It is shivering because of the cold wind. (d)
I bring it home and gave it some warm milk. (e)
12.
Dialogue Completion: Fill in the blanks with appropriate tenses.
Rohan: Hey! What ________ (you/do) right now?
Sam: I ________ (prepare) for my upcoming math test.
Rohan: How much of the syllabus ________ (you/cover) so far?
Sam: I ________ (finish) algebra yesterday. By tonight, I ________ (complete) geometry too.
13.
Transformation Challenge: Rewrite the entire passage changing the tense from Present to Past.
"The sun rises in the east. Birds chirp in the trees. The farmer goes to his field and starts ploughing. He works hard all day."
14.
Creative Application: Write a short paragraph (50 words) about a memorable trip you took recently. You must use at least two verbs in the Simple Past tense and one verb in the Past Continuous tense. (Underline these verbs).
15.
Integrated Grammar Passage: Fill in the blanks with the most appropriate tense forms.
Long ago, there ________ (live) a king who ________ (be) famous for his wisdom. One day, a merchant ________ (come) to his court. The merchant complained that someone ________ (steal) all his gold coins while he ________ (sleep).
